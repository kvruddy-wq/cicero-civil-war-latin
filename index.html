Teacher Notes (for you)

  • Level: Latin 3 (Suburani Book 2, first half; Book 1 review)
  • Focus: Interpretive reading; grammar embedded in context (no explicit grammar questions)
  • Grammar: Indicative; indirect statement; participles; relative clauses; no subjunctives
  • Student task: Choose paths and justify choices by quoting Latin phrases from the text
  • Estimated time: 30–40 minutes
